About MicroDegree

MicroDegree is an edtech platform teaching coding and job-ready skills in Kannada at an affordable price. We empower students with industry-ready certification courses by getting their foundations strong imparted through local language and context.

Our Vision

Transforming student careers

Our Mission

To empower students from tier-2,3 & rural region with industry-ready skills and map them with right job opportunities

Road-Map

  • Courses on - Coding Assessment on online platforms
  • Aptitude & Soft Skills
  • Industry tie-ups for placements

Recognition

  • Discounted pricing for premium courses
